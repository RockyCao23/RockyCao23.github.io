Projects

Displayed below are some of my personal projects





Art Metal

Metalwork Project | Dec 2018

Created in my metalwork class, this is a balancing desk toy and a piece of art. The initial design brief was to create a balancing toy but with cylindrical weights on both ends, and a sheetmetal cutout as the balancing center piece. However, I took the extra step to replace one of the weights with a 3D person snowboarding instead.

Drafting and Design

900 sq.ft condo | March 2020

Created in my Drafting and Design class, the left-most image drawing displays a 900 sq.ft condo I designed and drew on AutoCAD. The condo consists of two bedrooms, two bath, and an office. The center image is a combination of four identical condos to make up an apartment floor. The right-most image is one of many orthographic drawings I have made.

Harmonograph

Bonus Assignment | May 2020

Created as a bonus assignment, I attempted to use natural oscillations to create art. Inspired by the harmonograph in Science World Vancouver, I decided to build my own harmonograph which consisted of using common household items and my bunk bed.
